What is freefinity ?

Far far away, in our dreams, there's a city. It has a story. It's one of its kind.

The freefinity project aims to build smarter cities which are not only smart in terms of tech, but also in terms of hassles they pose in front of people which live in them. We analyse how to make cities better, minimize traffic, pollution, and litter. How we can build our cities so that they are safe and not vulnerable to natural calamities.

WE BELIEVE IN GREAT IDEAS

Our Phase 1 involves performing analysis of huge datasets of taxis / cab services, and pollution in the New York City, probably one of the most sought after city in the world.

This project is open to anyone living in any city. We are striving to make this project as global and as collaborative as we can.

We perform big data analytics and are striving to develop state-of-the-art systems which will provide substantial results to build and plan new cities effectively.

Our Focus

We wish to tackle a variety of issues. The problems that we wish to solve can be broadly classified into these classes.

Traffic

The residents of most of our cities are left with no choice but to accept congestion & traffic as a way of life.

Pollution

The environment opens up opportunities, and our cities are instead moving towards becoming a closed can of dust.

Crime

Almost all of our big cities face this problem. As the city grows, its crime control gets weaker.

Agriculture

There are a lot of new mouths to feed. With increasing urbanization, we're moving towards a major problem.

Sanitation

As more people flock to cities, providing clean areas & ensuring a proper channel for waste management becomes a challenge
